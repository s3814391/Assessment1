Project Idea:

Introduction:

My idea is to create a mobile app that can allow customers to record their shift, automatically calculate the wages, and any other situation like bonuses, penalties. Customers can custom the rules of how they get paid is the customer have any bonus today? How many bonuses are given when customers have more than how many leads. How many schedulers rates customers have? Does it affect the bonus? Or the company can sign up for their company and set up their own rules. So, when the employees got paid, they know how much exactly they got paid.

Motivation:

During my part-time job, I notice there have some apps that can allow the company gives shifts to the employees, but no apps where you can record your shifts and calculate the wages or bonus at the same time. I think for the job that got paid hourly, is very easy to calculate wages. There is also a huge amount of market for those kinds of apps. Lots of retail businesses, like supermarkets, restaurants, even this app just cooperating with a big company like Coles, WWS there is also a huge amount of the market for this app.

Description:

For these Apps, there have two main ways of features. Customers can set up their own job situations, customer can mark their own shifts, get notifications when the shifts are coming, set up their own rules of how they got paid, like wages bonus. suitable for the employees whose company is not joining our app. Another way is customers can join the organization that their company already set up the rules, the company can give the shifts to employees and the wages are calculated automatically. So how does it work? The app should have an online database to store the data like the hourly rate for each employee, shifts, and other information. The company can enter the data themselves or the customer can enter their own situations. The company can set up the wage rules in the app, for example in my job if your meet the requirement of daily target you will get a bonus and your final bonus will time your schedule rates. So, in our app we can have a similar rule after the employee got their shifts, the app can calculate how much time the employee works for that day, and does the job have a non-paid lunch break? If yes, the system can minus the lunch break time. Does the job have a double-paid weekend? If yes, the hourly rate will be double, or other numbers. Is there any leave? Or paid leave? How many bonuses do employees get? The company can set up those kinds of settings in the system and employees’ wages can calculate automatically. With this app, the company can be easier to set up the wages for each of the customers, especially for the company have high amounts of employees. It can make the wages more automated and clearer for the employees let them know how each item affects the wages.
